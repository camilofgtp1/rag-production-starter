Product FAQ

Q: What is your product?
A: Our product is an enterprise software solution that helps businesses automate their workflow processes and improve operational efficiency.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: We offer three pricing tiers: Starter at $99/month, Professional at $299/month, and Enterprise with custom pricing. All plans include a 14-day free trial.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial with full functionality. No credit card required.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all plans. Contact support for assistance.

Q: How do I get started?
A: Sign up for a free trial at our website, then follow the setup wizard to configure your account and invite team members.

Q: What kind of support do you offer?
A: We provide email support for all plans, live chat for Professional and Enterprise, and dedicated account managers for Enterprise customers.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and comply with GDPR regulations.
